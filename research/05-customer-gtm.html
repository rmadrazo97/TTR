Customer & Go-to-Market

Beachhead: Spanish international-running 1–10-truck carriers. Hook: gasóleo profesional enrolment assurance as a trust signal; the money is the foreign VAT & excise they abandon. Channel: the Facebook / Telegram communities where drivers already live. Full detail in the Spain Pilot Plan.

5.1 What carriers do today

SegmentCurrent behaviourOur conversion angle
Owner-operator (1 truck)Local gestor / accountant — or nothingSelf-serve app + no-win-no-fee removes cost & effort

Money left on the table is described as "billions unclaimed annually," lost to missed deadlines, non-compliant invoices, and errors — the top-three self-filing failure modes. Owner-operators default to a local gestor or nothing; small fleets already buy reclaim from their fuel card and are the easiest to convert.

Why Spain first, not Poland?

Poland is bigger and more cross-border — the scale market for phase 2 (PL 177k / RO 68k companies). But Spain offers a domestic, low-complexity, recurring hook (gasóleo profesional) that Poland lacks, plus a proven consumer tax-app culture (Taxfix operates in ES) and warmer trust. It lets us validate the AI-invoice pipeline before the harder foreign-VAT play — then fan out to Poland/Romania for VAT volume.

5.4 Global reference models

US · IFTA + per-diem

Quarterly fuel-tax filed via self-serve SaaS (TruckLogics, ExpressTruckTax) with telematics import. Per-diem $69/day. Lesson: truckers self-serve if input is automated and output is deadline-safe.

Consumer · Taxfix

Mobile-first, expert-in-the-loop, DE/IT/ES, >€2B refunded. Lesson: simple mobile UX + human backstop beats DIY portals for low-literacy users. ES already trusts the pattern.

B2B · FastVAT/Edenred

Pre-financing in 2–3 days at 5–12%. Lesson & threat: at a comparable fee we must win on AI self-serve ingestion + broader coverage (excise + dietas) + no card lock-in / no minimum — not on headline price.

5.5 Trust & adoption — the playbook

SME barriers are documented: vendor distrust, cost sensitivity, low digital literacy, habit, data-accuracy fear. How we overcome them:

  1. No-win-no-fee. "You pay nothing unless we find you money." Kills perceived risk.
  2. Pre-finance the refund (2–3 days). A powerful adoption lever vs FastVAT/Edenred — but it is a working-capital / credit business; partner with a factoring provider and defer until post-POC (see Risks §7.1).
  3. Native language + human backstop. Spanish first — Taxfix's proven wedge.
  4. Peer testimonials inside the same Telegram/FB group. Social proof beats ad spend here.
  5. "We handle the deadline." The 30 Sep VAT cutoff and gasóleo census are concrete, fear-based hooks.
  6. Fuel-card integration auto-ingests structured invoices — removes the data-entry barrier that kills DIY.
