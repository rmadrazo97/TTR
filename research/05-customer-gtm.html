5.2 First market ICP

ICP · first market

Spain Autónomo + micro, 1–10 trucks intra-EU international ≥3.5t (≥7.5t excise) Spanish-language

Hook: assure gasóleo profesional enrolment (trust) → monetise foreign VAT + excise.

Why Spain first, not Poland?

Spain · now — a domestic, recurring hook (gasóleo) that Poland lacks; Taxfix-proven tax-app culture; warmer trust. Validates the AI-invoice pipeline first.

Poland · phase 2 — bigger & more cross-border (PL 177k / RO 68k companies); the foreign-VAT volume play, once the pipeline is proven.

5.4 Global reference models

US · IFTA + per-diem

Truckers self-serve when input is automated and output is deadline-safe.

Quarterly IFTA via SaaS (TruckLogics, ExpressTruckTax) + telematics import · per-diem $69/day.

Consumer · Taxfix

Simple mobile UX + human backstop beats DIY portals — and ES already trusts the pattern.

Mobile-first, expert-in-the-loop · DE/IT/ES · >€2B refunded.

B2B · FastVAT/Edenred

Threat: at a comparable fee, win on AI ingestion + broader coverage (excise + dietas) + no card lock-in / no minimum — not price.

Pre-financing 2–3 days at 5–12%.
