Transportation Tax Recovery · Market Research

Recovering the tax that Europe's small carriers overpay — automatically.

An AI-native platform that lets individual truck drivers and small/medium EU road-freight carriers reclaim the fuel VAT, professional-diesel excise, and cross-border income-tax relief they are legally owed — by forwarding a photo, not hiring an accountant.

The thesis in one line

Incumbents (fuel-card majors + reclaim agents) serve fleets through account managers and card lock-in. Nobody serves the 1–10-truck owner-operator well. We win with self-serve AI ingestion, no-win-no-fee pricing, and a bundle no one else offers: fuel VAT + diesel excise + per-diem relief in one place.
