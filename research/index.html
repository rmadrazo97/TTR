Transportation Tax Recovery · Market Research

Recovering the tax that Europe's small carriers overpay — automatically.

A platform that lets individual truck drivers and small/medium EU road-freight carriers reclaim the fuel VAT, professional-diesel excise, and cross-border income-tax relief they are legally owed — by forwarding a photo of a receipt, not hiring an accountant.

Full source list and confidence flags on the Market Sizing page.
The thesis in one line

Incumbents serve fleets — fuel-card majors and reclaim agents, via account managers and card lock-in — while the owner-operator's local gestor files the domestic books but never chases the cross-border reclaim. So nobody turns the 1–10-truck operator's receipts into recovered tax. We win with self-serve AI ingestion, no-win-no-fee pricing, and a bundle no one else offers: fuel VAT + diesel excise + per-diem relief in one place.
