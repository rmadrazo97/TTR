WHY
HERE
Spain's whole market is our ICP

81% of freight firms own ≤5 vehicles · ~360,000 trucks (287,489 authorisations, +3.7% YoY) · aging, non-digital-native buyers (avg age >50, half >55 — trust & simplicity beat slick UX) · driver shortage 20–30k+, so recovered € is real money.

Aim first: Murcia · the Med corridor (Valencia/Cataluña) · Aragón — they over-index on long-haul international runs, where foreign-VAT recovery pays most.

Tailwind — a digitisation wave

Verifactu & Crea-y-Crece e-invoicing (2026–27) and the DECA transport document (late 2026) push this exact cohort to digital — nearer-term than EU ViDA (~2030).

THE
MONEY
Gasóleo is the hook; foreign VAT + excise is the money

Gasóleo profesional is already automated (card → AEAT weekly, refund monthly) — a perfect trust hook ("we make sure you capture your €2,450/truck"), but not the money. The money is the manual, abandoned foreign VAT + excise on international runs, plus dietas IRPF. Sell the assurance; monetise the recovery.

Per int'l truck: ~€4,000 fuel VAT + ~€1,200 excise + ~€800 toll VAT = ~€6,000 recoverable. At a 15% no-win-no-fee → ~€900/truck. Pilot = 25–40 carriers (~60–120 trucks) → €360–720k recovered, ~€55–110k fee — enough to prove the two load-bearing numbers.

Domestic IVA on national diesel/tolls is an ordinary modelo-303 deduction, not "recovery." Ignore the +€0.20/L temporary gasóleo aid (22 Mar–30 Jun 2026); the durable rate is €0.049/L.

WHO
The international-running autónomo — in an open field

ICP: an international-running autónomo / micro-carrier (1–10 heavy trucks, ≥7.5t) in Murcia / the Med corridor / Aragón, holding a fuel card + Certificado Digital, using a generalist gestor, leaving foreign VAT & excise unclaimed. Screen for: estimación directa VAT regime · ≥7.5t + Euro class (Italy needs Euro 5+) · a clean fuel-card invoice structure (§2.2), not already net-invoiced.

WK 8
GO?
The decision
Go — build

All three gates green (rights granted · ≥€4k/truck · fee accepted) → build the MVP (§6.1) — self-serve PWA + the modelo-360 flow, Spanish-first — and open the Fenadismer / fuel-card partnership, the P1 "partner for data" rung (§5.3).

No-go / fix-first

If certificate/apoderamiento friction blocks sign-up, fix that first — it's the true gate (G2). If €/truck or WTP miss, revisit the eligibility screen and fee level before any durable build.

WHAT
BREAKS
Spain-specific risks
Certificate / apoderamiento friction blocks non-digital-native drivers.→ hand-held in-person/video onboarding; consider colaborador social status
Gasóleo already automated — thin domestic wedge.→ monetise foreign recovery + dietas; gasóleo as trust/assurance only
Gestor loyalty — drivers trust their asesor.→ position as complement; offer gestor referral/rev-share
Regional gestorías (FROET) already bundle services.→ win on foreign automation + price + self-serve they lack

Cross-market risks (clawback, fraud, pre-financing, licensing) live in the §7.1 risk board.
